You in Africa

Would you like to change the world? With only €20 you can feed 100 children and make their world better


About

You in Africa started in 2018 when Lucas Tilhaqui went to Kenya and faced the reality of extreme poverty and precarious conditions in which people lived. He could not just watch it, so he decided to take take action.

The project would never have happened if Lucas had to change all this sadness reality on his own, so with help from some friends they started everything.

You in Africa started feeding 20 children per day in Thika and currently feeds more than 400 children every day in 4 different communities. The project also provides education, recreation, and healthy.

Volunteering

Why become a volunteer

1 - Volunteering Feels Good: Help other people make you better and forget about your own problems

2 - Volunteering Can Be Good for Your Career: When you give your time to other people, you show you take action to make the changes you want in the world. Also, show empathy and resilience.

3 - You Get Paid Back With Love: What is priceless

Volunteer Program

1 - The only cost you will have is US$10 per day, which includes 3 daily meals, accommodation, and laundry

2 - There is no limit of days to stay, you always will be welcome

3 - We pick you up and bring back to the airport

If you would like to know anything more about the program in more details, please contact us

* Sarafi tours are optional and on account of the volunteer

How become a volunteer

1 - Choose the dates

2 - Buy the tickets to the Nairobi Airport in Kenya

3 - Let the team knows the chosen dates and times

4 - Now it is time to get your hands dirty and make the difference
