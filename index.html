About

You in Africa started in 2018 when Lucas Tilhaqui went to Kenya and faced the reality of extreme poverty and precarious conditions in which people lived. He could not just watched it, so he decided take action.

The project would never have happened if Lucas had to change all this sadness reality on his own, with help from some friends he started everything.

You in Africa started feeding 20 children per day in Thika, and currently feeds more than 400 children everyday in 4 different communities. The project also provides education, recreation, and healthy.
